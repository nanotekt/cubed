Tax and Fee Policy as of 23 October 2023  
GreenArrays, Inc.  
GreenArrays is not in the business of collecting taxes, tariffs, or any other fees for any governments that  
deny our company representation but would like us to expend unreimbursed labor and expenses for their  
benefit. The number of tax collecting entities in the world is greater than any small business is capable of  
keeping up with; in order to spend our energies on the purposes for which our business exists, we have at  
this time no other choice than to set policies that minimize our effort in complying with laws as they  
become applicable to us, and to ignore inapplicable laws. This is especially important because these  
governments are continually trying to, and sometimes succeeding at, broadening the scope of their  
authority to compel tax collections by businesses that have nothing to do with them. Here, then, are our  
policies as of the date shown above.  
Sales to our Local Area(s)  
We presently have bona fide business presence, maintain offices, and do business in the City of  
Cheyenne, Laramie County, State of Wyoming, in the United States of America.  
We will collect Sales Tax on products delivered anywhere in Wyoming at the same rate payable by our  
company in Cheyenne. Wyoming does not tax shipping & handling.  
Sales to Other States  
At present, only one State (California) has compelled our company to collect its sales taxes, by  
threatening to prosecute our officers and directors who live in that state with misdemeanor crimes. As a  
result we collect California taxes on products delivered anywhere in California. Unfortunately, each city in  
California may have a different sales tax rate. Therefore, you will be charged sales tax at the current  
maximum rate of California cities. California taxes shipping & handling as well as product. UPDATE of  
23 Oct 2023: The relatively new business nexus rules in California presently excuse us from collecting  
sales tax in that State.  
For products delivered anywhere else in the US, you are responsible for paying any sales/use tax or  
equivalent as may be required by the State in which you receive delivery.  
Sales to Other Countries  
Taxes and fees, such as EU Value Added Tax and/or Tariffs, are charged against our chips and other  
products by many countries. We take no responsibility whatsoever for calculating, collecting, or paying  
these taxes and fees, nor for enforcing your payment of them, other than to use shipping methods that  
purport to require you to pay them before taking delivery of our products.  
What if we collect tax at a higher rate than actually applies to you?  
As is the case in California, we collect the maximum rate in the state, because there is presently no  
practical way to adjust to your local tax rate at the time of sale. If the rate charged to you is greater than  
the rate actually applicable to you, any remainder will remain with GreenArrays as a partial  
reimbursement for our labor and other costs in collecting and paying sales tax on shipments into your  
State.
